# 1. Chinese Solution

Egypt:

```text
III
```

3

---

Rome:

```text
III
```

3

---

China:

```text
三
```

3

---

ஒவ்வொரு number-க்கும் தனி symbol.

```text
一 = 1
二 = 2
三 = 3
四 = 4
五 = 5
六 = 6
七 = 7
八 = 8
九 = 9
```

# 10. Soroban (Japanese Abacus)

Now author introduces:

**Soroban**

---

Roman tabula:

Stones in grooves.

---

Soroban:

Beads on rods.

---

Like this:

```text
o
-----
o o o o
```

---

Upper bead = 5

Lower beads = 1

---

```text

```

Not step-by-step counting.
